1. Visualization Objective

The Executive Overview dashboard is designed to provide immediate visibility into the overall health of the loan portfolio.
The primary focus is on clarity, speed, and simplicity, enabling executives to understand key metrics at a glance without navigating detailed visuals.

2. Selected Visualization Type
KPI Cards (Text-Based Indicators)

All metrics in this dashboard are presented as KPI cards to emphasize headline values rather than analytical comparisons.

3. KPI-to-Visualization Mapping
KPI	Visualization Type	Rationale
Total Loans	KPI Card	Shows overall portfolio volume instantly
Total Customers	KPI Card	Highlights customer base size
Total Loan Amount	KPI Card (Currency)	Emphasizes financial exposure
Default Rate (%)	KPI Card (Percentage)	Clearly communicates risk level
Defaulted Exposure	KPI Card (Currency)	Quantifies financial impact of defaults
Average Interest Rate (%)	KPI Card (Percentage)	Summarizes pricing behavior

4. Why KPI Cards Were Chosen

Executives typically review dashboards in short timeframes

KPI cards present information without requiring visual comparison

Numeric indicators reduce cognitive effort

Metrics remain clear, consistent, and unambiguous

This approach ensures the dashboard remains focused on decision awareness rather than analysis.

5. What Was Excluded

Time-series visualizations

Segment-level breakdowns

Filters and interactive controls

These elements are addressed in separate analytical dashboards to maintain a clean and focused executive view.

6. Design Principles Applied

Minimalist design with a white background

Consistent font sizes and number formatting

No decorative visuals or unnecessary colors

Clear KPI labels that reflect business meaning

7. Alignment with Business Requirements

The chosen visualization approach directly supports the business objective of one-glance portfolio assessment, allowing leadership to quickly determine whether deeper investigation is required.
